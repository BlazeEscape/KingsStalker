About Our Charters

Premier Salmon Fishing on Lake Ontario

Operating a 28' Pursuit Sportfishing, Capt. Serge ensures a comfortable fishing experience in the waters of Toronto, Ontario. The crew will demonstrate techniques such as trolling, or something unique to the area. The only way to learn is to join them on the water.

Spend an unforgettable day on Lake Ontario with King Stalker Sport Fishing, led by the experienced and friendly Captain Serge Kulak. Surrounded by breathtaking views and the fresh open water, you and your family or friends can reel in King salmon, Lake Trout, and other exciting species. Whether you're a seasoned angler or casting a line for the first time, the adventure is filled with laughter, teamwork, and the thrill of the catch.

Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend bringing sunscreen, sunglasses, a hat, weather-appropriate clothing (layers are best), non-marking rubber-soled shoes, and any personal medications. We provide all fishing equipment and bait.

Yes, all anglers age 18 and older must have a valid Ontario fishing license. You can purchase one online at ontario.ca/fishing or at many local bait and tackle shops. Please bring your license with you on the day of your charter.

We require 3 days (72 hours) notice for cancellations to receive a full refund. If you cancel with less than 3 days notice or fail to show up for your charter, you will forfeit 100% of your payment. We fish in all weather conditions except when the Coast Guard issues small craft advisories, in which case we'll reschedule or refund.

The salmon run typically peaks from late May through September, with the largest fish often caught in August. However, we have good fishing from May through October, with different species being more active at different times. Our captains can advise you on the best timing based on what you hope to catch.
